Gaddi Dog

Type : Herding dog

An enormous mastiff-type breed originating from northern India, the Gaddi Kutta dog is sometimes referred to as the Indian Leopard hound or the Himalayan Sheepdog. Some people mistake them for the Tibetan Mastiff (most expensive dog) but the Gaddi Kutta is less bulky and has a mane like a big black lion. To the detailed observer, they stand out and are actually very different.

A Gaddi Kutta is not for the faint hearted. They are huge, aggressive, and incredibly strong. However, they are very intelligent dogs and can herd goats and sheep with little or no instruction.
