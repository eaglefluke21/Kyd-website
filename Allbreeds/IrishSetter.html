Irish Setter

Type : Family dog

Irish Setters originated as a gundogs in their native Ireland, and the dog breed‘s popularity soon spread, thanks to the beauty of their rich mahogany coat and their enthusiasm as bird dogs.

They’re friendly, mischievous, and inquisitive, with high energy levels. This is a bold and boisterous family friend with the potential to do well in many canine sports and activities.
